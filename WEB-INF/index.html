Where Have You Flown To

The Changes In America's Airport Passengers

Map Features

To scroll through the years use the slider bar located below the map. Other map features allow you to hover over the proportional symbols to receive information about the airport and zoom in and out of the map to receive a closer look. Also change the map theme from standard to physical in the upper right hand corner.

Statistical Comparison of Airports

Statistics from the Airports Council International displays information from 2006-2012 for passengers flying to and from United States and Canada each year. The map displays geographic consistency in variance of passengers between years. There was a decrease in flying from 2007-2009 which mimics the recession of the economy.

    Overall Percent Change in Passengers

  • From 2006 to 2007: 4% increase
  • From 2007 to 2008: -3% decrease
  • From 2008 to 2009: -4% decrease
  • From 2009 to 2010: 3% increase
  • From 2010 to 2011: 3% increase
  • From 2011 to 2012: 2% increase
